ESR Learning Management FAQs

This section provides access to Frequently Asked Questions when using the ESR Learning Management system.

I have completed some training and my compliance has not been updated

E-learning - Please ensure you have completed the E-Learning through a learning certificate, and you have clicked the house button to exit the course. Face to face/MS Teams - Please ensure you have waited 10 working days, for class sessions to be updated as these are updated manually. If your competencies has still not been updated please contact Training & Education on tewv.course.booking@nhs.net

Is mandatory training completed in another organisation recognised by TEWV?

Some training may differ from trust to trust however most of the training should be transferred when joining via the IAT process. If any training is incorrect or hasn't been transferred please email tewv.course.booking@nhs.net and we'll investigate this further or process any documentation, screenshots or learning records manually.

What do I do if my certification subscription status is "expired"?

Once your certification has expired you will need to renew the competency, if you're struggling to access this or have any queries please email tewv.course.booking@nhs.net.

Please ensure to complete all training shortly after you have initially accessed the course otherwise the competency could expire in the middle of you completing the training and entirely reset your progress. If this has occurred your line manager will have to email Education & Training to confirm you have completed all sections of the training.

My Compliance Portlet Showing Incorrect Requirements

Email tewv.course.booking@nhs.net with the details of the incorrect or missing requirement so they can review them on your behalf.

Compliance Mismatch Between ESR and IIC

If you are showing as green in your ESR matrix and red in the IIC, this could mean that the IIC has not updated or the requirement has not been added for you in the IIC. Please first allow three working days following completion of training for the IIC to update. If this has not updated, please log a call through the Service Desk portal querying why your competence has not updated. Please ensure in the call you state that ESR is showing as green. Basic Life Support (BLS)

Who should attend: For all staff who have access to emergency response bag and a duty to respond.

Learning Method: Face to face and competency assessment (3 hours 30 minutes).

Additional Information: This Training course covers both the practical and theory-based elements of Basic Life Support. This Course is required for all Staff who has a duty to respond and have access to the emergency response bag.

How to book: BOOK NOW

Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)

Who should attend: For all staff who don't have access to emergency response bag but have a duty to respond.

Learning Method: Face to face and competency assessment (1 hour).

Additional Information: CPR is a combination of artificial ventilation, chest compressions, the aim of which is to maintain effective ventilation and circulation until the arrival of the emergency services as this would help prevent irreversible brain damage and death.

How to book: BOOK NOW
